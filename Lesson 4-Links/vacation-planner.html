My Dream Trip to Rome

Ultimate Travel Guide

There are many things to see in Rome, Italy. The most popular atrraction is The Colosseum. However, Rome has many other attractions as well. These include the Trevi Fountian, Sistine Chapel, and the Catacombs. To find out more information, you could visit the Rome Tourism Website or the Tripadvisor Page For Rome, Italy. In addition, Rome has a Mediterranean Climate. For more information about the weather in Rome, visit Weather.com

Getting There & Staying There

Flight & Hotel Booking

Some important links for traveling include Booking.com and Expedia. These websites include important information on hotels and flights to our destination. They also allow us to book a reservation/buy a ticket.

Transportation Info

Rome has lots of public transportation. Their public transportation is managed by ATAC. You can find more information/pay for your transportation at atac.roma.it. Or, if you want, you could call and Uber, either using the Uber app or Uber's website.
